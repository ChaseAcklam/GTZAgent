Before we begin, you'll need a domain.

A domain name is the address where Internet users can access your website. It is beneficial if your domain name is similar to your company name.

At SalesDrip, we use and suggest a company named "GoDaddy" to register domains. When you select a domain name, GoDaddy will attempt to upsell you many different services. SalesDrip will be building and hosting your website, and providing your SSL certificate. We provide those services for you.

Your email address should closely match your website domain. We suggest using Gmail's business suite as opposed to others such as Microsoft Exchange. Gmail is a stronger product, a better value and has a proven successful track record from our experience.
